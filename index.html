Why Wireframe?

Wireframes are the blueprints of your site. They help you and your team understand the big picture and how multiple parts fit together. For you, rapid prototyping helps you explore more ideas, faster. For your team, wireframing helps you communicate high-level aspects of the design and get consensus early in the process. Don't get bogged down in unnecessary details too early. Iterating early is easier in the early stages.

Why Figma?

Traditionally, it's been better to use stand-alone wireframing tools that are entirely focused on making wireframes. With powerful and complex design tools like Figma, you can easily feel the need to make styling decisions that are too detailed. But with a proper wireframing kit, you can use all of Figma's powerful collaborative tools without sacrificing speed or efficiency.

I've used some of those wireframing tools, and I've always come across limitations. With Figma, that's something you don't have to worry about. By combining this kit with Figma's native features, you get prototyping, sharing, commenting, and real time collaboration — no more jumping between tools.
